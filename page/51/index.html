How to speed up ruby bundle gems fetch

01.01.0001
Short answer: use https://github.com/geminabox/geminabox for caching gems on your own host, or in your local network. I was using bundler’s vendor/cache in docker container, but with such improvement my container size grown up to 1,2Gb very quickly. Supply following settings to your geminabox container RUBYGEMS_PROXY=true https://medium.com/magnetis-backstage/how-to-cache-bundle-install-with-docker-7bed453a5800
